Frequently Asked Questions

How much does trip planning cost? +
Bespoke itinerary planning starts from £150 — a fully personalised day-by-day plan with accommodation recommendations and transport advice.
How long does it take? +
I'll send an initial itinerary within 24-48 hours. Most trips take 1-2 weeks from first contact to everything being confirmed and booked.
Do you only plan luxury trips? +
Not at all! I plan trips across all budgets — budget backpacking to five-star luxury honeymoons. The goal is always the same: best possible experience for your money.
Can you help with last-minute bookings? +
Yes — I'll always do my best. For the best selection, 4-8 weeks notice is ideal, especially for popular destinations in peak season.
Do you offer travel insurance? +
I don't sell insurance directly but provide honest guidance on the best policies for your trip type. I strongly recommend comprehensive cover for all travellers.
