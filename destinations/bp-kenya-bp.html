Kenya

The world's greatest safari, the Great Migration, Maasai culture, and the Indian Ocean coast — all in one country.

Africa

Why backpack Kenya?

Kenya is the world's original safari destination and still the best. The Maasai Mara during the Great Migration (July–October) is one of earth's most spectacular wildlife events. But Kenya is much more than the Mara — there's Mount Kenya to climb, the Swahili coast to explore, Samburu's red desert, and the Maasai culture to engage with respectfully and deeply.

Backpacker Highlights

8 Things to Do in Kenya

The experiences that make the trip — from the essential to the unexpected.

01
Game drive in the Maasai Mara during the Great Migration
02
Climb Mount Kenya — Africa's second-highest peak — via the Sirimon route
03
Visit a Maasai boma (village) and learn about traditional culture
04
Take the historic Mombasa-Nairobi overnight train (Madaraka Express)
05
Snorkel the coral reefs at Watamu Marine Park on the coast
06
Visit the Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage in Nairobi at feeding time
07
Watch cheetah hunt on the open Mara plains
08
Eat nyama choma (roasted goat) with ugali at a local joint
