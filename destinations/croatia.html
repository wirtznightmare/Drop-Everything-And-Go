Croatia

Adriatic island-hopping, ancient walled cities, and some of Europe's clearest water.

Europe · Croatia

Why visit Croatia?

Croatia has graduated from hidden gem to must-visit, and for good reason. The Dalmatian coast stretches over 1,000 islands, its water is an impossible shade of turquoise, and cities like Dubrovnik and Split contain some of the best-preserved medieval architecture in Europe. Skip the August hordes and visit in May, June, or September — you'll have the same scenery with a fraction of the crowds.

Is This Trip Right for You?

⛵

Island Hoppers

Hvar, Vis, Korčula, Brač — each island has its own personality. Hop between them by catamaran or private boat.

🏛️

History Lovers

Diocletian's Palace in Split is literally a Roman emperor's retirement home that became an entire city.

🤿

Outdoor Adventurers

Sea kayaking around Dubrovnik's city walls, cliff jumping in Hvar, and diving in Vis's crystal waters.
