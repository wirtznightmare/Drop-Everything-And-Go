Brazil

The Amazon, Rio's Carnaval, Iguazú Falls, Salvador's music, and some of the world's greatest beaches.

South America

Why backpack Brazil?

Brazil is enormous, chaotic, beautiful, and overwhelming in the best way. It's not the cheapest destination in South America but it's arguably the most exhilarating — a country of staggering natural wealth, extraordinary cultural energy, and a warmth that's infectious. The Amazon is a world in itself. Rio is a city unlike any other. And the beaches of the northeast are among the world's finest.

8 Things to Do in Brazil

01
Experience Carnaval in Rio or Salvador in February — nothing on earth compares
02
Hike to Christ the Redeemer at dawn and have it to yourself
03
Take a jungle boat trip deep into the Amazon from Manaus
04
See Iguazú Falls from both the Brazilian and Argentine sides
05
Sandboard the Lençóis Maranhenses dunes — lagoons between desert dunes
06
Explore the colonial gold-rush city of Ouro Preto
07
Party in Lapa, Rio — all night samba and caipirinha
08
Take the overnight bus north to the beaches of Jericoacoara
