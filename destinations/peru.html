Why visit Peru?

Peru is one of those rare destinations that exceeds every expectation. Machu Picchu is genuinely more impressive in person than in photographs. But Peru is far more than one site: the Sacred Valley, Cusco's colonial streets, the Nazca Lines, the Amazon basin, and one of the world's great emerging food scenes in Lima.
