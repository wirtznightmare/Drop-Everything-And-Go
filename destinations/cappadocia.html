Hot air balloons at dawn over fairy chimneys — one of earth's most otherworldly landscapes.

Asia · Turkey

Why visit Cappadocia?

Cappadocia looks like it belongs in a fantasy novel. Millions of years of volcanic eruptions and erosion created a landscape of extraordinary "fairy chimneys" — tall, thin spires of rock — which ancient Christians then hollowed into cave churches and underground cities. Now, hot air balloons drift over the valleys at sunrise in one of the most photographed spectacles on earth. It's genuinely like nowhere else.
